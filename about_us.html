Our Story

Founded in 2024, BHUKK emerged from a simple yet powerful idea: to transform how India experiences food delivery. What started as a small team of food enthusiasts has grown into a technology-driven platform serving thousands of customers daily.

Our journey is marked by continuous innovation, from introducing AI-powered recommendations to developing real-time tracking systems that set new industry standards.
