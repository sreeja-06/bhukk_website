Licensing FAQs

How do I upgrade my license?
You can upgrade your license at any time through your restaurant dashboard. The new rate will be prorated for the remaining billing period.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, UPI, and bank transfers. Enterprise clients can arrange custom payment terms.
Can I cancel my license?
Yes, you can cancel your license at any time. Refunds are provided on a pro-rata basis for unused periods.
