Refund Policy

Our Commitment

At Bhukk, we are committed to ensuring your satisfaction with our service. If you're not happy with your order, we're here to help make it right.

Refund Eligibility

You may be eligible for a refund in the following situations:

  • Order not delivered within the estimated delivery time + 30 minutes
  • Food quality issues (must be reported within 30 minutes of delivery)
  • Incorrect or missing items in your order
  • Order cancelled before restaurant confirmation

How to Request a Refund

  1. Log into your Bhukk account
  2. Go to 'Order History' and select the relevant order
  3. Click on 'Report an Issue' button
  4. Select the reason for your refund request
  5. Provide necessary details and submit any supporting photos
  6. Submit your request

Refund Processing Time

Once your refund is approved:

  • Bhukk Wallet credits: Instant
  • Credit/Debit cards: 5-7 business days
  • UPI/Net Banking: 2-3 business days

Need Help?

Our customer support team is available 24/7 to assist you with refund-related queries.
