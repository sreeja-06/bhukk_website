Refund Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2025

We strive to make every BHUKK experience perfect. When things don't go as planned, here's how we make it right.

1. Refund Eligibility

BHUKK is committed to customer satisfaction. You may be eligible for a refund in the following situations:

  • Order not delivered
  • Significant delay in delivery
  • Food quality issues
  • Incorrect items received
  • Missing items from order

2. Refund Process

To request a refund:

  1. Report the issue within 24 hours of delivery
  2. Use the "Help" section in our app
  3. Provide order details and reason for refund
  4. Submit photos if applicable (for quality issues)

3. Refund Methods

Payment Method Refund Time Refund Type
Credit/Debit Card 5-7 business days Original payment method
UPI 1-3 business days Original UPI ID
Wallet 24-48 hours Wallet balance
Cash on Delivery 1-3 business days Bank account/Wallet

4. Non-Refundable Cases

Refunds may not be available in these situations:

  • Customer-induced delays in delivery
  • Incorrect delivery address provided
  • Personal taste preferences
  • Requests made after 24 hours
  • Partially consumed orders

5. Cancellation Policy

Order cancellation terms:

Before Restaurant Accepts

Free cancellation available

During Preparation

Partial refund may be available

After Dispatch

No refund available

6. Special Circumstances

We may provide refunds in special cases:

  • Technical issues during ordering
  • Restaurant closures
  • Extreme weather conditions
  • System-wide outages
